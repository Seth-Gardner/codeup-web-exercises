How big are all of the National Parks?
All of the Parks combined are 52.2 million acres, or 81,250 square miles. Thats almost the size of Romania!
Can you hunt in National Parks?
As general rule, no. There are some exceptions though, for example Native populations in Alaska still hunt whale and seal as their main sources of food.

How many geysers are in YellowStone?
There are 500 geysers in Yellowstone, but there are more than 10,000 hydrothermal features scattered throughout the park.
